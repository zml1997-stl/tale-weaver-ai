How It Works

Creating a New Story

  1. Click "Generate New Story" on the homepage.
  2. Choose a genre or enter your own story idea.
  3. Select one of the generated story starters.
  4. As the story progresses, choose from the options provided to shape the narrative.
  5. When you're ready to end the story, click "End Story" to generate a final ending.

Loading a Previous Story

You can load and revisit any of your previously generated stories by clicking "Load Previous Story" on the homepage.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Use headphones for the best text-to-speech experience.
  • Try different genres to explore various storytelling styles.
  • Experiment with custom ideas for unique storylines.
